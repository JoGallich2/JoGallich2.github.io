About Me

My name is Joseph Gallichio, a junior at the University of Missouri-Columbia (Mizzou), where I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a minor in Information Technology. I am also working toward certifications in Cybersecurity and Information Systems and Technology to broaden my expertise.

Outside of academics, I serve as the Competitions Manager for the Rocket League esports club at Mizzou. In this role, I lead the Gold Team to national competitions, including NACE and NECC, and help coordinate tournaments and team strategies. Through my involvement in esports, I have developed strong leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. Along with that, my team and I have reached a rank of top 0.2% of players globally. My work in esports has taught me the importance of teamwork, adaptability, and clear communication.

I have hands-on experience with C++, Python, Java, and SQL, which I apply to my academic projects and personal endeavors. I am also passionate about building custom PCs, as it allows me to combine my love for technology and hands-on problem-solving. As I continue my education, I am eager to deepen my technical skills and make meaningful contributions in both technology and esports. I always make sure to approach challenges in coding, gaming, and teamwork with curiosity and determination.
