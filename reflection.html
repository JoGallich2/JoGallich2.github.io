Looking Ahead

My Reflection

Looking back on my time as a Computer Science major at the University of Missouri–Columbia, my academic journey was shaped by a broad list of courses that gave me a foundation in technology, engineering, and communication. Through my classes, I gained knowledge in algorithms, game development, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. All of these courses greatly increased my passion for both coding and problem-solving. My experiences in classes like Algorithm Design and Game Development sharpened my skills, and my participation in the Rocket League Esports Club taught me how valuable teamwork, leadership, and strategic thinking are.

Now that I've graduated, I'm building on that foundation as a Data Analytics Engineer Intern at Delta Defence, where I apply the skills I developed throughout my academic journey to real SQL and data-quality problems. I'm actively looking for full-time opportunities in software engineering, data, and cybersecurity, and I'm excited to keep working alongside people who challenge me to think more creatively, while I continue bridging the gap between my academic foundation and the day-to-day requirements of the industry.

My involvement with the Mizzou Rocket League Esports Club ran through the rest of my time on campus. As Team Captain and Competition Manager, I helped foster teamwork, leadership, and strategic improvement across the roster. The club was both a platform for growing my leadership skills and a source of inspiration in my academic work. My long-term goal is to keep excelling in my career while giving back to the community — whether that means mentoring new members of the club or the next generation of developers.

Looking forward to my career goals, I'm applying what I learned to tackle complex challenges in software engineering, data analytics, and cybersecurity. I'm also excited to continue exploring the combination of game development and artificial intelligence, areas where I hope to contribute to innovations on both ends. Ultimately, my career goals revolve around blending my passion for technology, gaming, and cybersecurity to create a meaningful impact in our society. Whether as a software engineer, data analytics engineer, or game developer, I strive to keep growing both personally and professionally, with the goal of contributing to advancements in the field and potentially mentoring the next generation of tech innovators.
